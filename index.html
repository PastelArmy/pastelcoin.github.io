🎯 About

PastelCoin ($NATA) is a community-driven memecoin inspired by Portugal’s iconic pastel de nata — a symbol of sweetness, tradition and delight.

$NATA is global by design. This is a project for pastry lovers, meme dreamers, nata maximalists and crypto degenerates from every corner of the internet.

Built on the BNB Chain, $NATA is fully transparent: no presale, no team allocation, and no hidden taxes. 100% of tokens were minted at launch and are visible on-chain.

Our mission? To bring flavour, fun and community to the crypto space — and maybe even let you buy a real pastel de nata someday using your $NATA bag.
